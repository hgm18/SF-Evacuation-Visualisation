  • Visualisation

To use the visualisation, use the "Place Horizontal Wall" and "Place Vertical Wall" buttons to place obstacles on the canvas, to demonstrate how the pedestrian experiences a repulsive force due to a boundary.

  • A black circle indicates a pedestrian
  • A red line indicates an exit
  • A orange arrow indicates the force experienced by a pedestrian due to the exit
  • A blue arrow indicates the force experienced by a pedestrian due to surrounding walls
  • A grey line represents a wall
Note: Due to the simplified Social Force model employed in this visualisation model, the pedestrians aren't very smart, and depending on your canvas set-up may never reach the exit.
