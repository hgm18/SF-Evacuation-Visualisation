  • Visualisation

Select how many pedestrians you want to have in the room in the textbox (large numbers may run slowly/break model). Click on a location on a wall to create an exit (multiple exits may be created). Click on "Start" to run the visualisation. The arrows denoting the 3 forces acting on a pedestrian can be toggled with the "Show/Hide Forces" button.

  • A black circle indicates a pedestrian
  • A red line indicates an exit
  • A orange arrow indicates the force experienced by a pedestrian due to the exit
  • A blue arrow indicates the force experienced by a pedestrian due to surrounding walls
  • A green arrow indicates the force experienced by a pedestrian due to another pedestrian
  • A grey line represents a wall
